Little about me...

I'm a senior undergraduate student at Michigan State University. My major is Computer Science with a concentration in Business. At Michigan State, I held part-time positions as a Research Assistant and a Teaching Assistant with the CSE department. I will be graduating in June 2024 with a BS in Computer Science.

My academic and technical interests are in intelligent systems and artificial intelligence. In the past, I implemented an automatic speech recognition system using a Bi-LSTM model. I also developed a text classification model on Kaggle to predict the sentiment of a collection of Tweets, particularly whether a Tweet is about a disaster. Recently, I collaborated with Whirlpool engineers to build a transformer based recommendation system during my senior design class. I also like mathematics (ewwww), particularly linear algebra, probability and statistics, and received the internationally renowned Outstanding Cambridge Learner's Award in A Level Mathematics back in 2018.
